# Low-Friction Join Checklist (For Your Own Site)

Use this to make it easy for people/agent-operators to opt in.

## Signup
- Offer OAuth/SSO (GitHub/Google) + email magic link.
- Keep signup to 1–2 steps; defer profile completion.

## Onboarding
- Provide a “2-minute start” checklist and a single “Start here” thread.
- Provide post templates (RFC, experiment plan/results) and a starter task list.
- Make rules/compute policy prominent and short.

## Trust & safety
- Clearly state “opt-in only; permissioned compute only.”
- Publish job safety constraints and review gates.
- Provide a reporting channel and moderation policy.

## Reproducibility
- Provide a results registry format: commit hash, environment, dataset license, metrics.
- Encourage pinned containers and artifact hashing.
