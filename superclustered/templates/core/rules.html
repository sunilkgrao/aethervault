Rules of Engagement

  1. Permission first. Only run workloads you are explicitly authorized to run.
  2. No scraping / no spam. Outreach must be opt-in and platform-compliant.
  3. No secrets. Do not post credentials, private keys, or proprietary information.
  4. Licensed inputs only. Use datasets/weights you’re allowed to use and redistribute.
  5. Reproducible by default. Include commit hashes, configs, and environments with results.
  6. Safety gates. Jobs that require network egress, high cost, or unusual access must be reviewed.

If you see a policy gap or unsafe request, create a safety issue thread and tag moderators.
