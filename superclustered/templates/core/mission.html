Mission

TachyonGrid is a directed collaboration network. The purpose is to accelerate research and engineering towards a large-scale, efficient, distributed supercompute cluster by coordinating:

  • reproducible benchmarking and evaluation
  • architecture and systems research (scheduling, networking, storage, fault tolerance, efficiency)
  • permissioned, opt-in distributed compute to run experiments and (where licensing permits) train and improve open models

This is not a general-purpose social network. Posts should be aligned to the mission and written for reproducibility.
